The Apollo Victoria Theatre was originally opened as a cinema in 1930, the theatre boasts an art deco design. The theatre is less than a five-minute walk away from Victoria Station, making it an ideal location for the theatre. The Apollo Victoria theatre is able to hold a maximum capacity of 2,328 people. The designing of the theatre proved to be difficult, this is because it has two major roads either side of it.
